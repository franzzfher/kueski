## 6. Scenario Analysis

## 7. Final Recommendations to CEO/CFO

### Recommendation: Adopt a Hybrid Approach

#### 1. Acquisition Policy Changes
* **IMMEDIATELY suspend lending to Risk Band 5 (Very High Risk).**
    * *Rationale:* -27.3% net margin and 34.4% loss rate.
    * *Potential Savings:* $444K.
* **SIGNIFICANTLY reduce lending to Risk Band 4.2.**
    * *Rationale:* -23.3% net margin and 31.9% loss rate.
    * *Potential Savings:* $572K.
* **MAINTAIN current acquisition for Risk Bands 1-3.**
    * *Rationale:* These segments are near break-even or profitable.

#### 2. Pricing Policy Changes
* **INCREASE rates for Risk Band 4.1 by 5-8 percentage points.**
    * Current: 24.5% ‚Üí Target: **30-32%**
    * *Rationale:* This is more achievable than the 38.7% break-even rate.
* **MAINTAIN current rates for Risk Bands 1-3.**

#### 3. Operational Improvements
* **FOCUS collections efforts on 0-30 DPD bucket.**
    * Early intervention can prevent roll to later stages.
* **REDUCE CAC for Low Risk segments.**
    * Current CAC of $72-135 is too high for loan sizes.
    * Target: <$50 for Low Risk (1-2).

#### 4. Expected Impact
* **Projected Improvement:** +$800K to +$1M annually
* **Target Portfolio Net Margin:** +5% to +8%
* **Reduced Loss Rate:** From 18.8% to <8%

## Conclusion
The current portfolio is unsustainable with a -12.11% net margin rate. The evidence clearly supports the CFO's position that changes are needed. However, a complete abandonment of high-risk segments may be too aggressive.

A measured approach that **eliminates the worst-performing segment (Risk Band 5)**, **significantly reduces exposure to Risk Band 4.2**, and **increases pricing** for remaining high-risk loans will position KueskiPay for sustainable profitability while maintaining market presence.
